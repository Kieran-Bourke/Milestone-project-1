Overview

Electric guitars are usually a next step up from a acoustic guitar as they tend to have a higher price tag and take a bit more understanding to use. This is not because they are inherintley more complicated infact the knowledge needed to use the actuall guitar is the same as for an acoustic, however they do require a amp to access its full potential. This can become confusing as you have more control on the excat tone that the guitar produces so I would reccomend it to people who have played guitar before or have a big interest in soley electric sounds.
